# Ashguard Compact

The Ashguard Compact is a coalition of gate captains, oath-bound surveyors, and veterans hired to keep commerce moving.

## Public Role

- Escorts caravans through disputed roads.
- Settles market violence before it becomes family violence.
- Maintains signal fires between [[Daggerheart/Age of Umbra/World/Locations/Amber Reach|Amber Reach]] and the lower terraces.

## Tension

The Compact insists it serves the city. Everyone else keeps asking which version of the city it means.
