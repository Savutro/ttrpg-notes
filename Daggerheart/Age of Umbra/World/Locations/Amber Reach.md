# Amber Reach

Amber Reach is the public face of Shadowfall: wind markets, cliff lifts, lantern courts, and warehouses that never quite sleep.

## What Players Know

- The Reach controls most legitimate food and medicine entering the city.
- The oldest lifts are maintained by families who treat repair routes as inherited secrets.
- Pilgrims leave strips of amber cloth on the railings before crossing into colder districts.
