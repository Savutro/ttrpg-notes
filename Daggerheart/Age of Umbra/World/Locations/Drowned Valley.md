# Drowned Valley

The Drowned Valley lies below the city terraces, where broken milestones disappear into reed beds and cold mist.

## Features

- Bell posts mark the safest crossings when the fog is thick.
- Ruined milehouses still show tax marks from a kingdom nobody names in daylight.
- The water reflects stars even under cloud cover.

## Rumors

- Ferrymen claim the valley remembers every oath sworn above it.
- A dry stair has appeared near the western levee three nights in a row.
