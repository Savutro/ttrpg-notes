# Kiera Vale

Kiera Vale is a courier who knows which alleys flood first and which watch captains accept apologies before coin.

## At A Glance

- Practical, fast-talking, hard to impress.
- Carries two ledgers: one honest, one useful.
- Trusts anyone who can name three safe roofs between here and the river.

## Connection

Kiera can point the party toward [[Daggerheart/Age of Umbra/World/Locations/Amber Reach|Amber Reach]] when the route matters more than the destination.
